THIS IS ME

NUR FARHANA HANUM, June 1st, 2020

I was born on 11th of January as the second daughter of four siblings. Being raised up as one of the big sisters at home, challenges me to be matured at a very young age. Not only I get to learn doing house chores, I also learnt how to take care of my younger siblings as my parents were busy for work. It most likely looked like I was always in charge at home. As I growing up, I have always been observing people’s emotions. It’s a thing I always do subconsciously, really. The most common emotions that not everyone get to do is loving oneself. Loving ourselves.

MUSIC

FAVORITE SONGS, June 1st, 2020

I am a big fan of music. In my way, music could be an escaping place for me to run away from the noisy real life and just focus on one thing. Listening to music that was often played during a significant life event, such as a family celebration, or perhaps a breakup may leave some prints. It can trigger a deeply nostalgic emotional experience, and a musical scar. The feeling is not the music but the experience it reminds us of.
